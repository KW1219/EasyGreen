##Problem
As me and my partner have grown, we've seen a trend in the behavior of our peers. In our community, once students reach highschool, they start to care more about their physical characteristics whether it's beauty, weight, or physique. However, one shared concern amongst all these characteristics is the factor of diet. Some individuals, not just students, aim to eat more, and some aim to eat less. It doesn't stop there however, things become more complicated when specific nutrients are thrown into the equation: carbs, sugars, protein, dairy, and more. In addition, with the dominance of social media in our daily lives, its easy for the community to get confused or led to believe false information regarding proper diet. This can potentially lead to unwanted physiques long term or possibly even harmful and dangerous improper diets.

##Our Solution
The solution me and my partner came up with is an AI program that takes in the user's goals, restrictions, and health to come up with a schedule that tends to the specific needs of the user. The output from the AI is specifically built to be simple and easy to understand while being credible with ratings out of 100 to show how suggested the AI is with certain meals/meal plans. Also, the AI will provide summarized bullets of why a meal was suggested and links to scholarly research papers supporting the AI's claims (if possible).
